Frequently Asked Questions

Here are basic notes for first-time use and common questions about Stockout Zero.

What is Stockout Zero?

Stockout Zero is a simple incremental inventory game about checking stock, earning money, buying upgrades, and building automation toward zero stockouts.

Do I need an account?

No developer-operated account is required for the core game. Game Center is only used for leaderboards.

How do leaderboards work?

Stockout Zero uses Game Center for fastest clear rankings. Daily, weekly, and all-time leaderboards may be available depending on your Game Center status and region.

Why is my score not appearing?

Please check that you are signed in to Game Center and that network access is available. Game Center rankings may also take some time to update.

Can I request a feature?

Yes. Please send feature requests by email. Clear examples of your intended use are especially helpful.

Where can I check how data is handled?

Please refer to the Privacy Policy for information about local game data, Game Center, and contact handling.
