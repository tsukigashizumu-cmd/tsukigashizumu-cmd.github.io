Frequently Asked Questions

Here are basic notes for first-time use and common questions about JustThisOnce.

What is JustThisOnce?

JustThisOnce is a short psychological diagnosis game about small everyday exceptions, kindness, fairness, hidden costs, and uncomfortable consequences.

Do I need an account?

No developer-operated account is required for the core game. Game Center is only used for achievements.

How do achievements work?

JustThisOnce uses Game Center for achievements such as completing a diagnosis, unlocking endings, and reaching specific results. Achievement updates may take some time depending on your Game Center status and network connection.

Why did an achievement not appear?

Please check that you are signed in to Game Center and that network access is available. Game Center achievement status may also take some time to update.

Can I request a feature?

Yes. Please send feature requests by email. Clear examples of your intended use are especially helpful.

Where can I check how data is handled?

Please refer to the Privacy Policy for information about local diagnosis data, unlocked endings, Game Center, and contact handling.
