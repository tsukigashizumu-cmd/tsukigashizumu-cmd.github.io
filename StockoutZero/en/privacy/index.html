Stockout Zero Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Stockout Zero is designed to be simple and privacy-friendly. This page explains how data collection, Game Center leaderboards, external services, and contact information are handled.

Basic Policy

Stockout Zero does not collect personal data through a developer-operated server. The app is intended to work as a simple local game, with Game Center used only for leaderboard features.

1. Data collected by the developer

The developer does not operate a dedicated server to collect personal data from Stockout Zero users.

  • No developer-operated account system
  • No developer-operated user profile database
  • No developer-operated analytics server
  • No advertising SDK used by the developer

2. Local game data

Stockout Zero may save game progress, settings, and related gameplay information locally on the user's device. This local data is used to provide the app's functionality and restore the game state.

3. Game Center

Stockout Zero uses Apple's Game Center only for leaderboard features. Game Center may process information such as scores, leaderboard entries, player identifiers, and related Game Center data according to Apple's policies and user settings.

The developer does not use Game Center data to create a separate user profile outside Apple's Game Center service.

4. Ads and tracking

Stockout Zero does not include third-party advertising SDKs and does not use third-party tracking for advertising purposes.

5. In-app purchases

Stockout Zero does not currently offer in-app purchases. If this changes in the future, payment processing and purchase information management will be handled through Apple's systems.

6. External services

Stockout Zero may use Apple services necessary for app distribution, Game Center leaderboards, crash reports, and platform operation. These services are provided by Apple and are subject to Apple's own policies and user settings.

7. Contact

If you contact the developer for support, the information you provide in that inquiry may be used only to respond to your request.

8. Changes to this policy

This policy may be reviewed and updated as necessary. If there are important changes, they will be announced in the app or on related pages.

9. Effective date and last updated date

Effective date: May 19, 2026
Last updated: May 19, 2026
