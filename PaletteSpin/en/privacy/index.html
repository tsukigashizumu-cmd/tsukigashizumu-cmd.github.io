PaletteSpin Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

PaletteSpin is designed to be used with confidence. This page explains how user-entered content, locally saved data, ads, external services, analytics, and contact information are handled.

Basic Policy

PaletteSpin handles information only within the scope necessary to provide, improve, and support the app.

1. User-entered content and locally saved data

PaletteSpin is a color palette app that may allow users to create, adjust, and save color-related settings or creative ideas. Data needed for the app's local workflow may be saved on the user's device.

  • Color palette settings and related adjustment values
  • Saved color ideas or app settings used to restore the app state
  • Information needed to provide app functionality on the device
  • Information that third-party services may obtain for ad delivery, measurement, or platform operation

The developer does not operate a dedicated server to collect the actual color ideas or palette settings users create in the app. Information handling may be updated depending on the specifications of external services and platforms used by the app.

2. Ads

PaletteSpin may use third-party advertising services to deliver ads. These services may obtain information for ad display, fraud prevention, and performance measurement.

Please also refer to the policies of each service provider for information about how ad-related data is handled.

3. In-app purchases

PaletteSpin may offer in-app purchases. Payment processing and purchase information management are handled through Apple's systems.

4. Analytics and measurement

PaletteSpin may use analytics or measurement tools to improve app quality, understand issues, and review usage status.

5. External services

PaletteSpin may use external services related to ad delivery, measurement, in-app purchases, app distribution, crash reports, and app operation. Please refer to each provider's policy for information about how data is handled by external services.

6. Contact

For inquiries about this policy, please contact us using the information listed on the support page.

7. Changes to this policy

This policy may be reviewed and revised as necessary. If there are important changes, they will be announced in the app or on related pages.
