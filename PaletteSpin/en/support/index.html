Frequently Asked Questions

Here are basic notes for first-time use and common questions about PaletteSpin.

What is PaletteSpin?

PaletteSpin is a simple color palette app for exploring color combinations, adjusting the look, and saving creative ideas for later.

Do I need an account?

No. The core local workflow does not require an account.

Can I save palettes?

PaletteSpin is designed to help you keep color ideas and settings you like so you can come back to them later.

Why do colors look different on another screen?

Color appearance can vary depending on device display, brightness, export format, and the apps or services where the colors are used.

Can I request a feature?

Yes. Please send feature requests by email. Clear examples of your intended use are especially helpful.

Where can I check how data is handled?

Please refer to the Privacy Policy for information about data handling.
