What PaletteSpin helps you do

PaletteSpin is designed for quick color exploration. It keeps the workflow light so you can try ideas without getting stuck in complicated settings.

Explore color combinations

Try different color groups and discover combinations that feel useful for wallpapers, icons, layouts, mood boards, and visual experiments.

Adjust the look

Fine-tune colors and presentation options so the palette feels closer to the image or mood you want to create.

Save ideas for later

Keep palettes and settings you like so you can come back to them when you want to continue working on an idea.

Use it as a creative starting point

PaletteSpin is useful when you want a small spark before creating an image, choosing a theme, or deciding the direction of a design.

Built for lightweight creative decisions

Choosing colors can become surprisingly slow when there are too many options. PaletteSpin focuses on making that first step easier: try, compare, adjust, and keep the ideas that feel right.

Useful for

  • Finding a color direction for a small project
  • Creating palette ideas for wallpapers or visuals
  • Comparing different moods before committing to one
  • Saving color ideas for later reference

A small note before you use it.

PaletteSpin helps with creative color exploration, but final color appearance may vary depending on your device display, brightness, export format, and the apps or services where you use the colors.

Start with a spin. Keep the colors that feel right.
