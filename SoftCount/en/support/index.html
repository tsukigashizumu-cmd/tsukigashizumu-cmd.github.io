Frequently Asked Questions

Here are basic notes for first-time use and common questions about SoftCount.

What is SoftCount?

SoftCount is a gentle counting and breathing timer for following calm rounds, simple phases, and steady rhythm.

Do I need an account?

No. The core local workflow does not require an account.

Is SoftCount a medical app?

No. SoftCount is intended as a general counting and breathing support tool. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, or emergency support.

Can I use it for breathing-style routines?

Yes. SoftCount can be used as a simple visual guide for repeated breathing-style phases, such as inhale, hold, exhale, and rest.

Can I request a feature?

Yes. Please send feature requests by email. Clear examples of your intended use are especially helpful.

Where can I check how data is handled?

Please refer to the Privacy Policy for information about data handling.
