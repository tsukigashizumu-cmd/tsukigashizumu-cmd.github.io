SoftCount Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

SoftCount is designed to be used with confidence. This page explains how locally saved data, user settings, ads, external services, analytics, and contact information are handled.

Basic Policy

SoftCount handles information only within the scope necessary to provide, improve, and support the app.

1. User settings and locally saved data

SoftCount is a general counting and breathing support app that may allow users to configure counting rounds, timing phases, display settings, or related preferences. Data needed for the app's local workflow may be saved on the user's device.

  • Counting, timing, or phase settings
  • Display preferences and app settings used to restore the app state
  • Information needed to provide app functionality on the device
  • Information that third-party services may obtain for ad delivery, measurement, or platform operation

The developer does not operate a dedicated server to collect the actual counting settings or breathing-style routine settings users create in the app. Information handling may be updated depending on the specifications of external services and platforms used by the app.

2. Health and medical information

SoftCount is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, or emergency support. The app is intended only as a general counting and breathing support tool.

Users should not rely on SoftCount for medical decisions or emergency situations. If you have health concerns, please consult a qualified professional.

3. Ads

SoftCount may use third-party advertising services to deliver ads. These services may obtain information for ad display, fraud prevention, and performance measurement.

Please also refer to the policies of each service provider for information about how ad-related data is handled.

4. In-app purchases

SoftCount may offer in-app purchases. Payment processing and purchase information management are handled through Apple's systems.

5. Analytics and measurement

SoftCount may use analytics or measurement tools to improve app quality, understand issues, and review usage status.

6. External services

SoftCount may use external services related to ad delivery, measurement, in-app purchases, app distribution, crash reports, and app operation. Please refer to each provider's policy for information about how data is handled by external services.

7. Contact

For inquiries about this policy, please contact us using the information listed on the support page.

8. Changes to this policy

This policy may be reviewed and revised as necessary. If there are important changes, they will be announced in the app or on related pages.
