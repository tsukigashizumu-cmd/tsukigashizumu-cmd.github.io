What SoftCount helps you do

SoftCount focuses on making a count feel easy to follow. It is designed for quiet repetition, simple breathing rounds, and soft visual guidance.

Follow a calm count

Use SoftCount as a simple guide when you want to move through repeated counts or timed phases without relying on a cluttered screen.

Support breathing rhythm

SoftCount can help you stay with a steady rhythm for breathing-style routines, including repeated phases such as inhale, hold, exhale, and rest.

Keep the screen gentle

The app is designed around soft colors, clear numbers, and simple movement so the count remains readable without feeling harsh.

Use it as a small reset

SoftCount can be used as a short visual companion when you want to pause, count, breathe, or return to a steadier pace.

Built for simple, low-stress repetition

Some timers feel too mechanical, and some breathing apps feel too heavy. SoftCount keeps the experience minimal so the count can stay central.

A small note before you use it.

SoftCount is intended as a general counting and breathing support tool. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, or emergency support.
