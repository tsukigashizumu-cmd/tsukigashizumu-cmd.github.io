What is JiiyaRoll?

Roll the dice for 24 hours.

Roll the dice six times and see whether you get a 6. If that is all you do, you can call it luck.

But what if you have 24 hours to keep rolling? Before you look for a reason you failed, just roll once more. If you are tired, take a break. If your environment is holding you back, improve it. And if your own hands are not enough, make someone else roll for you.

JiiyaRoll is a simulation game where you roll dice, earn money, and increase the number of attempts you can make. Hire Jiiya workers, improve your environment, take breaks, and find out what remains after 24 hours.

JiiyaRoll Features

This is a dark casual simulation game about building a system that increases attempts, instead of simply waiting for luck.

Six rolls are luck. 24 hours are structure.

At first, all you can do is roll by hand. But across 24 hours, your choices for increasing attempts can change the final result.

Earn money and hire Jiiya

Earn money by rolling dice, then hire Jiiya workers to increase your attempts. But cheap labour is cheap for a reason.

Keep going with breaks and upgrades

Protect your wrists, reduce fatigue, and improve your environment so you can keep rolling. Attempts do not increase through grit alone.

Game Center competition planned

Planned Game Center support includes total attempts, final money, daily and weekly leaderboards, and achievements for your 24-hour results.

What remains after 24 hours?

Was it luck, effort, or a system you built so you could call it effort? JiiyaRoll is a game about that blurry boundary.

You cannot choose luck

You cannot choose whether a 6 appears. But you can build the number of rolls, and the environment that makes them possible.

Kindness, or sunk cost?

Is keeping a Jiiya who can no longer work an act of kindness? Or is it just your inability to cut your losses? The choice is yours.

Sweet offers

Sometimes a choice appears that lightens the burden in front of you, in exchange for giving something up. Is it salvation, a deal, or a sweet whisper?

A game about building structure

What did you really need: willpower, a system, or the resolve to cut something away? That is what it means to keep rolling.

Who it is for

JiiyaRoll is for players who enjoy luck-based games, clickers, idle games, incremental progression, and dark management simulations.

You like building up attempts

  • You enjoy dice, probability, and unpredictable swings
  • You like clicking, idling, upgrades, and progression
  • You want to see the result of a full 24-hour run

You like slightly dark simulations

  • You enjoy decisions about hiring, running away, and cutting losses
  • You want to look behind self-improvement-style words
  • You want to play with the boundary between effort and systems
