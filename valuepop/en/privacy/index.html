ValuePop Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

ValuePop is designed to be used with confidence. This page explains how user-entered content, data saved on your device, ads, external services, and contact information are handled.

Basic Policy

ValuePop handles information only within the scope necessary to provide, improve, and support the app.

1. User-entered content and saved data

ValuePop does not collect the actual content that users enter into the app. Product names, prices, quantities, fixed-amount discounts, percentage discounts, and registered entries are saved on the user's device as data needed to use the app.

  • Input data used in the app, such as registered products and comparison entries
  • Input data for comparison, including multiplied quantities and discount conditions
  • Information needed to check or restore purchase status
  • Information that third-party services may obtain for ad delivery or measurement

The developer does not externally transmit or collect the actual content users enter in the app. Information handling may be updated depending on the specifications of external services and platforms used by the app.

2. Ads

ValuePop may use third-party advertising services to deliver ads. These services may obtain information for ad display and performance measurement.

Please also refer to the policies of each service provider for information about how ad-related data is handled.

3. In-app purchases

ValuePop may offer in-app purchases. Payment processing and purchase information management are handled through Apple's systems.

4. Analytics and measurement

ValuePop may use analytics or measurement tools to improve app quality, understand issues, and review usage status.

5. External services

ValuePop may use external services related to ad delivery, measurement, and app operation. Please refer to each provider's policy for information about how data is handled by external services.

6. Contact

For inquiries about this policy, please contact us using the information listed on the support page.

7. Changes to this policy

This policy may be reviewed and revised as necessary. If there are important changes, they will be announced in the app or on related pages.
