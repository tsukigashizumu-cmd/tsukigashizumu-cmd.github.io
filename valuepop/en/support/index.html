Frequently Asked Questions

Here are basic notes for first-time use and common questions about Cheaper Choice features.

Can I temporarily turn off ads?

Yes. When needed, you can turn off ads for a limited time by watching a rewarded ad.

Will my registered entries remain?

Yes. Registered entries can remain available for continued use. You can also save them and load them later to review past entries or return to a familiar setup.

Can I delete the initial data?

Yes. The initial demo data can be cleared so you can start from scratch. If you are unsure, you can save a backup before organizing or deleting entries.

Does Cheaper Choice support discounts?

Yes. Cheaper Choice supports percentage discounts and fixed-amount discounts, making it easier to compare the actual value beyond the displayed price.

Does it support multiplied quantities?

Yes. Cheaper Choice can handle cases where multiple quantities are multiplied together. Built-in help is available when you need guidance.

Where can I check how data is handled?

Please refer to the Privacy Policy for information about data handling.
